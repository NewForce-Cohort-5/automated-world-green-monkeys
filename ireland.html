Ireland

From passage tombs older than the pyramids to cities founded by Vikings, crumbling castles to grand country estates, Ireland's history is etched on its landscape. Invaded and settled by the Celts, the Vikings, the Normans, the English and the Scots over thousands of years, they have all left their mark on its history, geography, culture, language and people.
